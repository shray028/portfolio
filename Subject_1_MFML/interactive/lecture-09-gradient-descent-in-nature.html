The Mountain & Local Minima

Momentum Local Minima

Observe the trail left by the ball.
• Standard: Gets stuck in the first small valley (Local Min).
• Momentum: Uses accumulated speed to climb over the hill and find the true bottom (Global Min).

The Gradient Vortex

Oscillation Convergence

Like water draining or a black hole, gradients pull towards the center. Notice how particles speed up as they get closer to the solution (the center), often overshooting and spiraling in.

Stochastic Search (Lightning)

Greedy Search Pathfinding

Lightning finds the ground by making rapid, greedy decisions. It doesn't know the best path beforehand; it just blindly picks the path of least resistance at every micro-step.

Gas Entropy

Exploration Equilibrium

High energy systems naturally seek low energy states (Equilibrium). By compressing the gas (High Cost) and releasing it, the system automatically "searches" for the most stable configuration.
